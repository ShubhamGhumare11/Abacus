Introduction to MAGIC TABLES

Learn to make tables up to 999 without multiplications

Many children struggle with learning their multiplication tables–as their parent, you may feel like it’s your duty to help them. After all, they’ll need quick skills to help them throughout high school, college, and life.

Therefore, we have come up with a special course for kids aged 8 to 12 years to teach them tables from 2 to 999 without doing any multiplication. Usually, multiplication takes a lot of time, is boring, and at times, frustrating. But we teach a special method by which kids can give answers to any table up to 999 in just seconds.

One of the key advantages of using an abacus is its ability to enhance mental calculation skills. By manipulating the beads on the abacus, users can visualize and understand mathematical concepts more easily. This hands-on approach improves calculation speed and helps develop a deeper understanding of mathematical operations.
